Edson Express
Commerce City, CO
MC-035227 DoT 604
This was a large LTL carrier out of Denver that was a western carrier
and moved a lot of freight between Denver and the Midwest and the West Coast.
Was owned and run by Jake Edson and in the late 1980' or early 1990's closed
