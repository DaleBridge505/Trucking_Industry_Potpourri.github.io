Closed down 2009 At the time of its closing (12-22-2009) , Can-Truck employed about 60 owner/operators. Can-Truck’s head office was in Oshawa and it operated satellite terminals in St. Catharines, Ont., Belleville, Ont. And Livonia, Mich.
